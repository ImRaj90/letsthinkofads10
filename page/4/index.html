Introduction to Display Advertising

2016-08-14

With the rapid growth in the technology, internet usage has increased exponentially through out the world. The main reasons for using internet is for communicating, availability of information etc,. which made software technology to grow even faster. Many companies started provide their services through internet. The companies which used to provide their services traditionally through offline are now also providing their services through online. In a short period, users or customers have started leveraging these online services such as banking services, industrial services, newspapers services etc,.

Advertising industry is not an exception, The main concept behind advertising is to increase brand awareness. Traditionally the advertising is done through Hoarding, Newspapers, Radio and Television etc,. Every medium has there own advantages and disadvantages.

Newspapers:
Newspapers are best way to reach country or city audience. We can target audience of a single country or city etc,. But there is no accurate count of how many people have saw the advertisement and how many of them are converted.

Hoardings:
Hoardings are also called as Billboards. Billboard is a large outdoor advertising structure typically found in high-traffic areas such as alongside busy roads. Billboards present large advertisements to passing pedestrians and drivers.

Disadvantages:
Lack of accurate count of how many people have saw the advertisement and how many of them are converted.

Radio:
Radio is traditional way of advertising through air where advertising are broadcasted on regular intervals.
Disadvantages:
Lack of voice clarity.
Lack of accurate count of how many people have saw the advertisement and how many of them are converted.

Television:
Through television advertising, advertisement can done through a short videos explaining about the brand.
Disadvantages:
Lack of accurate count of how many people have saw the advertisement and how many of them are converted.

The key participants in the advertising are:

  • Advertiser
  • DSP
  • Agency
  • SSP
  • Publisher

There are different ways of marketing or advertising such as

  • Display Advertising
  • Native Advertising
  • Search Marketing
  • Affiliate Marketing

Display Advertising
Ads which displayed on websites are considered as the display advertising. It may be either Video, Audio, images, Text, etc,.

Search Marketing
SEM deals showing the ads on top and left side of the google search results.

Affiliate Marketing
Affiliate Marketing is king of marketing where the publisher will get a certain percentage of commission for selling the advertiser product.
