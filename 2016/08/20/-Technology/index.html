Ad Technology

2016-08-20
Rajashekar Jangam

The term “Ad Tech” also refers to the Advertising Technology, which leverages programmatic buying and selling of advertisement. With the help of the Ad Tech, the advertiser can reach and make profits by targeting there audience accurately based on the demography and geography.

Before Ad Tech coming into the picture, for displaying the ads on the publisher website, there used to be a lot of manual work is done by the ad networks. Lets dive into history of Ad networks.

In the beginning, the advertiser used to approach the publishers or the publishers used to approach the advertisers to advertise advertiser’s website or product on the publisher website.

The advertisers never knew how many impressions will be displayed on the publisher website because there were no analytics available. The advertiser used to spend their money without any proper metrics.

The publisher website got more popular and more advertisers began to request to advertise their products or websites on the publisher website.

The single advertiser is looking for multiple publisher website to reach more audience and gain more profits.

But there were no analytical tools which shows the metrics between the advertiser and publisher. There was big gap between these two parties. To bridge the gap between the advertiser and publisher, ad Networks came into existence.

In simple words, Ad network is a network of publishers and advertisers where are advertisers buy the adspace from the publisher website. The advertisers come up with budget, targeting audience for serving ads on the publisher websites. Then the ad networks look for the targeting options for the advertiser.
