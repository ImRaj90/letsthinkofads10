Real Time Bidding

2016-08-20
Rajashekar Jangam

Real Time Bidding(RTB) is a programmatic way of bidding for each ad impression based on the various factors such as demographic information, geography information, browsing history, etc,. Real Time Bidding takes less 100 milli seconds to display an ad on the publisher website.
